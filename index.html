    About Sloths

    Sloths are nocturnal animals. And despite what most people think sloths only sleep for 10 hours a day. Since their diet consists of mostly leafs, they don't have that much energy throughout the day. And when they are hanging from a branch and start to climb, they sometimes grab onto their own arm thinking its a branch and fall to their death. Its sad but true. They can also continue to hang onto the branch they were on after death.
